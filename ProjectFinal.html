Hello visitor, welcome.

Tauras Koreiva is a 3D Environment Artist living in Nottingham, England.

He currently works for Criterion Games as a World Artist II, although previous roles have included 3D Environment Artist roles at XR Games, Playground Games, and Sumo Digital.

He specialises in asset creation and scene composition, but also enjoys contributing to art direction, mentoring juniors, and troubleshooting.

Softwares Used:
3DS Max. Maya. ZBrush. Substance Painter. Unity.
