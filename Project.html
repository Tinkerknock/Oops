Tauras Koreiva

World Artist II at Criterion Games


ArtStation Portfolio

Tauras Koreiva is a 3D Environment artist living in Nottingham, in the United Kingdom.

He currently works for Criterion Games as a World Artist II, although previous roles have included 3D Environment Artist roles at XR Games, Playground Games, and Sumo Digital.

He holds a Bachelor's degree in Game Art from the University of Hertfordshire.
