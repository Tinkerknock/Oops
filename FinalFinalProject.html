Tauras Koreiva is a 3D Environment Artist living in Nottingham, England.


He currently works for Criterion Games as a World Artist II, although previous roles have included Senior Environment Artist at XR Games, Asset Artist at Playground Games, and Environment Generalist at Sumo Digital.

He specialises in asset creation and scene composition, but also enjoys contributing to art direction, mentoring juniors, and troubleshooting.

He's worked on critically acclaimed first party XBOX game Forza Horizon 5, but has also shipped other titles such as Team Sonic Racing.

Softwares Used:
3DS Max. Maya. ZBrush. Substance Painter. Unreal Engine. Blender. Substance Designer. Marmoset Toolbag.


Outside of work, he enjoys sim racing, 3D modelling and printing, and tolerating his girlfriend Savannah.
